3D Function Grapher

Instructions:

Just enter a function in the input at the bottom.

Some supported operations and their symbols are:

  • Addition + , Subtraction - , Multiplication * , Division /
  • Exponents ^
  • Exponents ^
  • Factorial !
  • Remainder %
  • Absolute value abs()
  • Sine sin() , Cosine cos(), Tangent tan()
  • Arc sine asin() , Arc cosine acos(), Arc tangent atan()
  • Square root sqrt()
  • Cube root cbrt()
  • Log base 10 log10()
  • Log base 2 log2()
  • Natural log ln()
  • Random value random()
